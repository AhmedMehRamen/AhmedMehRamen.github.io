What is anime

Anime is hand-drawn and computer animation originating from Japan. Anime, a term derived from the English word animation, in Japanese describes all animated works, regardless of style or origin. Outside of Japan, anime refers specifically to animation produced in Japan or to its common style, which has since been adopted by a minority of works produced in other countries.
